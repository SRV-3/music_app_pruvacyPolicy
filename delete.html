Delete Account

We're sorry to see you leave. You can permanently delete your Himza account directly from the mobile application.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Himza app.
  2. Go to Profile.
  3. Tap Settings.
  4. Select Delete Account.
  5. Read the warning carefully.
  6. Confirm your identity if prompted.
  7. Tap Delete Permanently.

⚠ Important

Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. Your playlists, liked songs, preferences and all associated account data will be permanently removed.

Need Help?

If you're experiencing an issue or have any questions, please contact our support team before deleting your account.
